# Hero Design — Expanded Kit & Identity

> Mobile top-down action roguelite with magic pylon defense. One session = 2-4 minutes (map completion).

---

## 1. Knight

### Core Fantasy
Disciplined frontliner who controls space around pylons with precise strikes and short-range crowd control.

### Playstyle Loop
Engage → cleave → reposition to next threat cluster → repeat. The Knight wants to stay *between* enemies and pylons, never chasing deep.

### Strengths / Weaknesses
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| Reliable AoE melee damage | No ranged poke |
| Good survivability via armor passive | Low mobility; punished by fast flankers |
| Simple execution floor | Damage ceiling lower than Berserker/Mage |

### Abilities

| Slot | Name | Cooldown | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A1** | **Cleaving Arc** | 3 s | Wide 180° slash dealing 100% ATK. Hits up to 6 enemies. Bread-and-butter waveclear. |
| **A2** | **Shield Bash** | 6 s | Short dash forward, stunning the first enemy hit for 1 s and knocking back others in a small cone. |
| **A3** | **Rally Banner** | 12 s | Plant a banner (lasts 5 s) that boosts nearby pylon damage by 20% and grants the Knight 15% move speed while inside. |
| **A4** | **Judgment Strike** | 18 s | Overhead slam in a small AoE. Deals 300% ATK and applies a 2 s armor-shred debuff (enemies take 25% more damage from all sources). |
| **Passive** | **Plate Training** | — | Incoming damage reduced by 12%. Stacks an additional 4% (max 3 stacks) for each enemy within melee range, rewarding the Knight for standing ground. |

### Session Flow (2-4 min)
0:00-0:30 — Use Cleaving Arc to thin early scouts; plant Banner near the first pylon.  
0:30-1:30 — Rotate between two pylons, Shield Bash to interrupt elite charges.  
1:30-2:30 — Elite/mini-boss wave: stack Plate Training in a crowd, drop Judgment Strike for armor shred, let pylons finish.  
2:30-3:30 — Boss phase: cycle Banner for pylon buff uptime, weave Judgment Strike on cooldown. Knight peaks when standing in a dense pack with full passive stacks + Banner active.

### Differentiation
Only hero with a pylon-buffing ability (Banner). Trades kill speed for team/structure utility.

## 2. Guardian

### Core Fantasy
Immovable protector who absorbs punishment and creates safe zones around pylons.

### Playstyle Loop
Taunt → absorb → Shield Nova to heal/repel → anchor the most threatened pylon.

### Strengths / Weaknesses
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| Highest effective HP | Lowest DPS of all heroes |
| Strong peel and taunt | Struggles to clear waves solo before they split |
| Excellent at stalling bosses | Relies on pylons for kill pressure |

### Abilities

| Slot | Name | Cooldown | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A1** | **Provoke** | 5 s | Taunt all enemies in a medium radius for 2 s, forcing them to attack the Guardian. |
| **A2** | **Aegis Wall** | 8 s | Project a short barrier (width ~3 tiles, lasts 3 s) that blocks enemy movement and projectiles. |
| **A3** | **Shield Nova** | 10 s | Pulse that deals 80% ATK, knocks enemies back, and restores 8% max HP to the Guardian. |
| **A4** | **Consecrate** | 20 s | Mark a zone (radius ~2.5 tiles, 6 s duration). Enemies inside are slowed 40% and take 50% ATK/s. Pylons inside gain a temporary shield equal to 15% of their max HP. |
| **Passive** | **Bastion** | — | While standing still for ≥1 s, gain 20% damage reduction. Resets on movement. Encourages anchoring. |

### Session Flow
Early: Provoke scouts into pylon fire. Mid: Place Aegis Walls at chokepoints, Consecrate the most pressured pylon. Late: Chain Provoke + Shield Nova to survive boss aggro while Consecrate melts adds. Peaks when anchored on a pylon with Bastion active and Consecrate ticking.

### Differentiation
Only hero with a hard taunt and structure shielding. Pure tank identity — zero burst, maximum control.

## 3. Berserker

### Core Fantasy
Reckless damage dealer who grows stronger as health drops, trading safety for explosive kill speed.

### Playstyle Loop
Dive → take damage intentionally → trigger rage abilities → obliterate pack → find the next pack before rage fades.

### Strengths / Weaknesses
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| Highest single-target and burst AoE DPS | Fragile; can die in seconds if mismanaged |
| Snowballs hard with Bloodlust passive | No pylon utility or team buffs |
| Fast clear speed | Poor at protecting multiple pylons simultaneously |

### Abilities

| Slot | Name | Cooldown | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A1** | **Rend** | 2.5 s | Fast double-slash dealing 120% ATK total. Applies bleed (30% ATK over 3 s). |
| **A2** | **Leap Slam** | 7 s | Jump to target location, dealing 150% ATK on impact. Initiator and escape. |
| **A3** | **Whirlwind** | 9 s | Spin for 2 s, dealing 60% ATK/s in a radius. Can move at 70% speed while spinning. |
| **A4** | **Execution** | 14 s | Single-target strike dealing 250% ATK + bonus 150% ATK if target is below 30% HP. Boss-killer. |
| **Passive** | **Bloodlust** | — | Below 50% HP: +25% ATK. Below 25% HP: +50% ATK and +15% lifesteal. High risk, high reward. |

### Session Flow
Early: Rend through scouts, take a few hits to dip below 50%. Mid: Leap Slam into dense packs, Whirlwind for AoE, Bloodlust amplifies everything. Late: Execute elites/boss on cooldown. Peaks at low HP with Bloodlust fully active, chaining Rend bleeds into Execution finishers.

### Differentiation
Only hero whose power *increases* as they take damage. Opposite philosophy to Guardian. Rewards aggression and HP management skill.

## 4. Archer

### Core Fantasy
Mobile marksman who kites enemies and picks off priority targets from safety.

### Playstyle Loop
Position at range → mark target → burst with piercing arrows → reposition with Tumble → repeat.

### Strengths / Weaknesses
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| Safe sustained damage at range | Low HP; dies fast if caught |
| Excellent priority-target elimination | Weak AoE without Volley cooldown |
| High mobility | Pylon defense is indirect (kill before they arrive) |

### Abilities

| Slot | Name | Cooldown | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A1** | **Piercing Shot** | 3 s | Arrow that passes through up to 3 enemies in a line, dealing 110% ATK to each. |
| **A2** | **Tumble** | 5 s | Quick dodge-roll granting 0.5 s invulnerability. Resets A1 cooldown on use. |
| **A3** | **Volley** | 10 s | Rain arrows on target area (radius ~2 tiles) for 3 s, dealing 40% ATK/s. Zone denial. |
| **A4** | **Hunter's Mark** | 12 s | Mark a single enemy for 6 s. Marked target takes 30% more damage from all sources and is visible through fog. |
| **Passive** | **Steady Aim** | — | Each consecutive shot on the same target increases damage by 8% (max 5 stacks, 40%). Resets when switching targets. Rewards focus-fire. |

### Session Flow
Early: Piercing Shot lines of scouts, stacking Steady Aim on tougher targets. Mid: Volley chokepoints, Tumble to stay safe. Late: Hunter's Mark the boss, stack Steady Aim to 5, burst with Piercing Shot. Peaks when stationary at range with full Steady Aim stacks on a Marked boss.

### Differentiation
Only hero with true ranged safety and single-target focus amplification. Highest skill ceiling for positioning and target selection.

## 5. Mage

### Core Fantasy
Spell-slinger who controls the battlefield with elemental AoE and devastating cooldown windows.

### Playstyle Loop
Zone with Frost Ring → charge up Meteor → detonate → manage mana/cooldowns during downtime → repeat burst window.

### Strengths / Weaknesses
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| Best AoE burst in the game | Long cooldowns create vulnerable windows |
| Strong zone control with slows/burn | Lowest base HP; no mobility skill |
| Pylon synergy via Arcane Link | Completely reliant on ability timing |

### Abilities

| Slot | Name | Cooldown | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A1** | **Arcane Bolt** | 2 s | Homing projectile dealing 90% ATK. Low cooldown filler between big spells. |
| **A2** | **Frost Ring** | 8 s | Expanding ring of ice that slows enemies by 40% for 3 s and deals 70% ATK. |
| **A3** | **Meteor** | 14 s | 1 s channel, then massive AoE impact dealing 350% ATK + 80% ATK burn over 4 s. |
| **A4** | **Arcane Link** | 16 s | Tether to a pylon for 6 s. While linked, the pylon's attacks deal 35% bonus magic damage and the Mage gains 20% cooldown acceleration. |
| **Passive** | **Resonance** | — | Every 4th ability cast releases a free Arcane Bolt at the nearest enemy. Rewards rapid casting. |

### Session Flow
Early: Arcane Bolt spam, Frost Ring to funnel scouts. Mid: Arcane Link a pylon, use CDR window to double-Meteor a wave. Late: Frost Ring → Meteor combo on boss adds, Link the nearest pylon for sustained DPS. Peaks during Arcane Link window with Meteor off cooldown and Resonance procs chaining.

### Differentiation
Only hero with pylon-tethering for mutual benefit and the highest raw AoE damage ceiling. Punished hardest by poor cooldown management.

## Balancing Guidelines for 2-4 Minute Sessions

### Cooldown Philosophy
- **Filler (A1):** 2-3 s. Used 40-60+ times per run. Must feel responsive.
- **Utility (A2):** 5-8 s. Available for every wave engagement (~6-10 uses per run).
- **Impact (A3):** 8-14 s. Meaningful choice per wave; ~4-8 uses per run.
- **Ultimate (A4):** 14-20 s. Defines peak moments; ~3-5 uses per run. Should feel *event-like*.

### Damage Tuning
- Total enemy HP budget per run: calibrate so the average hero kills the last enemy at ~3:00 assuming 80% ability uptime.
- Berserker at full Bloodlust should clear ~30% faster than Guardian; this gap is the balance envelope.
- Boss HP: tuned to survive 2-3 full Ultimate rotations from any hero.

### Passive Scaling
- Passives should provide 15-30% effective power increase when played well vs. ignored. High enough to reward mastery, low enough that a missed proc doesn't brick the run.

### Session Pacing Targets
| Phase | Time | Enemy Pressure | Hero Power |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ramp | 0:00-0:45 | Low (scouts) | Learning the map layout |
| Mid | 0:45-2:00 | Medium (mixed waves + elites) | Core rotation online, passives stacking |
| Peak | 2:00-3:30 | High (boss + adds) | All cooldowns cycling, peak decision-making |
| Overtime | 3:30+ | Escalating | Do-or-die; missed cooldowns = loss |

### Implementation Notes
- Store cooldowns/damage as data, not code — enables rapid tuning via config.
- Log per-hero clear times in playtests; flag any hero consistently ±20% off median.
- Passives should be stateless where possible (check current HP, count recent casts) to simplify save/resume.
