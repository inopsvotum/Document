# Hero Roster Expansion

Additional hero concepts for Magic Pylon. These heroes complement the base roster (Knight, Guardian, Berserker, Archer, Mage) by filling strategic gaps and offering fresh playstyles tuned for 2–4 minute sessions.

---

## Melee Heroes

### Lancer

**Role:** Melee / Skirmisher  
**Playstyle:** Hit-and-run engager. The Lancer excels at diving into clusters, dealing burst damage, and repositioning before retaliation. High skill ceiling — rewards players who read enemy movement patterns.

**Strengths:** Strong gap-close, excellent burst on isolated targets, high mobility.  
**Weaknesses:** Paper-thin defenses, poor sustained DPS, punished hard if caught mid-cooldown.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Momentum** | Passive | Each tile moved without stopping increases the Lancer's next attack damage by 8%, stacking up to 5 times. Stacks reset on attacking or standing still for 1s. |
| 1 | **Thrust** | Active | Dash forward 3 tiles in a line, dealing piercing damage to all enemies passed through. 4s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Sidestep** | Active | Instantly dodge 2 tiles perpendicular to facing direction, gaining 1s of damage immunity. 6s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Impale** | Active | Lunge at a single target within 2 tiles, dealing heavy damage and pinning them in place for 1.5s. 10s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** Only hero with true directional dodge. Momentum passive rewards constant movement, creating a distinctive "always sprinting" rhythm unlike any base-roster hero.

---

### Paladin

**Role:** Melee / Support Hybrid  
**Playstyle:** Frontline fighter who trades raw damage output for team-wide utility. Walks toward objectives steadily, sustaining nearby allies through auras and heals. Ideal for players who prefer a supportive anchor role.

**Strengths:** Strong sustain, aura buffs for nearby allies, good pylon defense.  
**Weaknesses:** Low damage output, no gap-closers, kitable by ranged enemies.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Consecrated Ground** | Passive | Tiles the Paladin walks over glow for 3s, granting 10% damage reduction to allies standing on them. |
| 1 | **Holy Strike** | Active | Melee attack that deals moderate damage and heals the Paladin for 20% of damage dealt. 3s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Divine Shield** | Active | Grant a nearby ally (or self) a shield absorbing damage equal to 15% of Paladin's max HP. Lasts 4s. 8s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Judgment** | Active | Slam the ground, dealing AoE damage in a 2-tile radius and slowing enemies by 30% for 2s. 12s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** The trailing consecrated tiles create a visible "safe path" for allies — a spatial support mechanic no other hero provides.

---

### Assassin

**Role:** Melee / Burst DPS  
**Playstyle:** Stealth-oriented glass cannon. Operates on the flanks, picks off low-HP targets, and vanishes. Excels in the final push toward pylons when defenders are weakened.

**Strengths:** Highest single-target burst in the game, stealth access, execute potential.  
**Weaknesses:** Extremely fragile, useless against grouped enemies, stealth broken by AoE.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Exposed Weakness** | Passive | Attacks against enemies below 30% HP deal 25% bonus damage. |
| 1 | **Shadowstep** | Active | Teleport behind a target enemy within 4 tiles, dealing damage on arrival. 5s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Vanish** | Active | Enter stealth for 2.5s. Next attack from stealth deals 50% bonus damage and breaks stealth. 10s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Eviscerate** | Active | Rapid 3-hit combo on current target over 0.8s, dealing high cumulative damage. Requires melee range. 8s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** Only hero with stealth. The execute passive combined with Vanish burst creates a "closer" role — Assassin is weakest at fight start but deadliest at the end.

## Ranged Heroes

### Crossbowman

**Role:** Ranged / Siege DPS  
**Playstyle:** Slow, deliberate ranged attacker with the highest per-shot damage among ranged heroes. Plants in position and punishes enemies that funnel through chokepoints. Rewards map knowledge and positioning.

**Strengths:** Extreme single-shot damage, long range, strong zone control.  
**Weaknesses:** Slow attack speed, immobile while charging, vulnerable to flanks.

**Ability Kit (2 Passives + 2 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P1 | **Heavy Bolts** | Passive | Basic attacks have 20% longer range than standard ranged attacks but fire 30% slower. |
| P2 | **Armor Pierce** | Passive | Attacks ignore 15% of target's damage reduction. |
| 1 | **Power Shot** | Active | Charge for 1.2s, then fire a bolt that pierces through all enemies in a line, dealing 200% damage. 7s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Caltrops** | Active | Scatter caltrops in a 3-tile area. Enemies walking through take minor damage and are slowed 40% for 2s. Lasts 6s. 12s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** Two passives create a distinct "heavy weapons platform" feel. Caltrops give self-peel that other ranged heroes lack, making the Crossbowman a one-person choke holder.

---

### Javelin Thrower

**Role:** Ranged / Bruiser  
**Playstyle:** Mid-range hybrid comfortable in skirmishes. Throws javelins at medium range but can hold their own in melee when closed on. Fluid transitions between ranged poke and melee brawling.

**Strengths:** Flexible engagement range, strong kiting, good 1v1 dueling.  
**Weaknesses:** Master of none — outranged by true ranged, out-sustained by true melee. Medium-range only.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Skirmisher's Stance** | Passive | After throwing a javelin, gain 15% movement speed for 1.5s. After a melee strike, gain 10% damage reduction for 1.5s. |
| 1 | **Hurl Javelin** | Active | Throw a javelin up to 5 tiles, dealing damage and reducing target's movement speed by 20% for 2s. 3s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Shield Bash** | Active | Melee-range bash that stuns for 0.8s and knocks the target back 1 tile. 6s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Rain of Spears** | Active | Throw 3 javelins in a cone, each dealing moderate damage. 9s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** The only hero explicitly designed to weave between ranged and melee. Skirmisher's Stance rewards constant mode-switching rather than committing to one range.

## Mage Heroes

### Necromancer

**Role:** Mage / Summoner  
**Playstyle:** Raises temporary undead minions from fallen enemies to overwhelm through numbers. Weak alone, devastating if allowed to snowball through a map. The "investment" mage — power ramps as enemies die.

**Strengths:** Snowball potential, action economy through summons, strong pylon pressure.  
**Weaknesses:** Useless until first kills, summons are fragile, AoE clears them instantly.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Soul Harvest** | Passive | Enemies killed within 4 tiles drop a soul orb. Collecting it restores 5% max HP. Max 2 orbs active at once. |
| 1 | **Raise Dead** | Active | Target a fresh corpse within 3 tiles to raise a skeleton minion (50% of original enemy's HP, 40% damage). Max 3 minions. Lasts 12s. 4s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Life Drain** | Active | Channel a beam at an enemy within 4 tiles, dealing damage over 2s and healing the Necromancer for 50% of damage dealt. 8s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Corpse Explosion** | Active | Detonate a corpse or skeleton minion, dealing AoE damage in a 2-tile radius equal to 30% of the original unit's max HP. 6s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** The only hero whose power is directly tied to enemy deaths. Creates a unique decision loop: raise the corpse for sustained pressure, or detonate it for burst AoE?

---

### Druid

**Role:** Mage / Shapeshifter  
**Playstyle:** Switches between caster form (ranged spells, healing) and beast form (melee, tanky). Flexible solo hero that adapts to whatever the map demands. High floor, moderate ceiling.

**Strengths:** Extreme adaptability, self-sustain, can fill any role in a pinch.  
**Weaknesses:** Weaker than specialists at any one thing, form-swap cooldown creates vulnerability windows.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Nature's Balance** | Passive | Switching forms restores 8% max HP. Internal cooldown: 10s. |
| 1 | **Shapeshift: Bear** | Active | Transform into bear form for 8s. Gain 30% damage reduction and melee attacks, but lose access to spells. 12s cooldown (starts after form ends). |
| 2 | **Entangling Roots** | Active | Caster form only. Root enemies in a 2-tile radius for 2s and deal minor damage over time. 9s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Rejuvenation** | Active | Caster form only. Heal self or an ally for 25% max HP over 4s. 10s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** Only hero with form-switching. The passive heal on swap incentivizes frequent toggling rather than camping one form, creating a rhythmic playstyle unlike any other hero.

---

### Chaos Warlock

**Role:** Mage / Risk-Reward DPS  
**Playstyle:** Channels unstable magic that hurts enemies and the Warlock alike. Highest magic DPS in the game, but every spell has a cost. For aggressive players who treat HP as a resource.

**Strengths:** Top-tier sustained magic DPS, AoE dominance, self-fueling damage loop.  
**Weaknesses:** Self-damage is real and lethal, no defensive tools, needs careful HP management.

**Ability Kit (1 Passive + 3 Actives):**

| # | Name | Type | Description |
|---|------|------|-------------|
| P | **Chaos Siphon** | Passive | 10% of all damage dealt to enemies is returned as HP to the Warlock. This is the only sustain source — there are no defensive abilities. |
| 1 | **Hellfire** | Active | Blast a 3-tile area, dealing heavy fire damage to all units inside — including the Warlock if caught in the zone. 5s cooldown. |
| 2 | **Blood Pact** | Active | Sacrifice 15% current HP to empower the next ability cast within 4s, increasing its damage by 40%. 7s cooldown. |
| 3 | **Chaos Bolt** | Active | Fire an erratic projectile at a target within 6 tiles. Deals massive damage but has a 20% chance to veer off course and miss. 4s cooldown. |

**Uniqueness:** The only hero that actively self-damages. Blood Pact into Hellfire is the highest damage combo in the game — if you survive it. The RNG on Chaos Bolt adds tension without being game-breaking at 4s cooldown.

## Roster Balance Summary

| Hero | Type | Role Niche | Complexity |
|------|------|-----------|------------|
| Lancer | Melee | Mobile burst skirmisher | High |
| Paladin | Melee | Supportive frontline | Low |
| Assassin | Melee | Stealth finisher | High |
| Crossbowman | Ranged | Siege / zone control | Medium |
| Javelin Thrower | Ranged | Hybrid skirmisher | Medium |
| Necromancer | Mage | Summoner / snowball | Medium |
| Druid | Mage | Shapeshifting flex | Medium |
| Chaos Warlock | Mage | High-risk glass cannon | High |

Combined with the base five (Knight, Guardian, Berserker, Archer, Mage), the full roster reaches **13 heroes** covering defensive tanks, aggressive bruisers, stealth assassins, ranged specialists, summoners, shapeshifters, and self-destructive mages — enough variety for meaningful draft choices in short sessions without bloating selection screens.
